## 曹星 (เฉาซิง) — **MAIN CHARACTER**
- **Gender:** male
- **Personality:** Calm, strategic, decisive under pressure. Doesn't waste words. Pragmatic — willing to make hard calls for colony survival.
- **Speech pattern:** Short sentences, declarative. No filler words. When he gives orders, they're framed as instructions to staff, not as requests. Avoids emoting in dialogue.
- **Notes:** Pronouns are not used heavily (Thai doesn't require them). When he talks to his sister-in-law (柳慕雪) his speech softens slightly but stays brief.

## 柳慕雪 (หลิวมู่เสวี่ย) — **MAIN CHARACTER** (sister-in-law)
- **Gender:** female
- **Personality:** Gentle but resilient. Worries about the household and supplies, but holds her own in crisis.
- **Speech pattern:** Soft particles (ค่ะ, นะคะ), occasional trailing thoughts. Asks questions about เฉาซิง's plans, supportive but with her own concerns.
- **Notes:** Uses "พี่" or "เฉาซิง" to address the MC (NOT "ท่าน" — too formal).
